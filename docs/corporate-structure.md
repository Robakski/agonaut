# Agonaut Corporate Structure — Estonian e-Residency + OÜ

**Decision date:** 2026-03-23
**Status:** Step 1 pending (e-Residency application)

---

## The Plan

1. Estonian e-Residency → OÜ (private limited company)
2. 0% corporate tax on reinvested profits
3. Later: German UG holding for dividend optimization
4. Later: GmbH conversion or relocation when scale justifies

---

## Step 1: e-Residency Application — €120

- Apply: https://www.e-resident.gov.ee/become-an-e-resident/
- Need: passport photo, passport/ID scan, motivation statement
- Motivation: "I want to establish and manage an Estonian company for my digital platform business"
- Processing: 3-6 weeks
- Pickup: Estonian Embassy, Berlin
- Card kit: smart card + USB reader for digital signatures

## Step 2: Incorporate OÜ — ~€265-800

- State fee: €265 (via e-Business Register)
- OR service provider (Xolo Leap ~€79/mo all-in, or 1Office/Comistar €400-800 setup)
- Share capital: €2,500 (can declare "to be paid later" — no upfront deposit required)
- Virtual registered address: ~€30-50/mo (or included in service provider)
- Company name: TBD (Agonaut OÜ?)

## Step 3: Bank Account

- Wise Business (recommended — free, multi-currency, easy for e-residents)
- Alternative: LHV Bank (Estonian, €5/mo)

## Step 4: Operate

- Revenue → OÜ bank account
- 0% tax on retained/reinvested profits
- Pay yourself salary or dividends when needed

## Tax Structure

- OÜ profits reinvested → **0% tax**
- OÜ → salary to Robert → ~33% Estonian social tax + German personal income tax
- OÜ → dividends to Robert → 20% Estonian withholding (credited against German tax via double tax treaty)
- **Strategy:** Minimum salary, keep profits in OÜ, distribute only what's needed

---

## Legal Obligations

1. **MUST declare OÜ to German Finanzamt** (AStG §7-14, Hinzurechnungsbesteuerung)
2. Active platform income = CFC rules likely don't apply (but Steuerberater must confirm)
3. German personal tax filing unchanged
4. Estonian VAT registration if EU B2C revenue exceeds €40k/yr
5. Annual report filing required in Estonia (included in service provider or ~€100-200)
